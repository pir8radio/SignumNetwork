Why Signum DEX?

Decentralized exchanges (DEX) are a cryptocurrency exchanges which enable secure and direct peer-to-peer transactions, and without the need for an intermediary.  

BTDEX is the decentralized exchange system on the Signum chain. It implements a unique non-custodial exchange method for cryptocurrencies and conventional fiat currencies based on Signum SmartJ Smart Contracts and on-chain encrypted messages.
